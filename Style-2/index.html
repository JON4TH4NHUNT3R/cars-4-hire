About Us

Cars 4 Hire can offer you a personalised service tailored to meet your needs.

After working for a number of the larger car hire companies, I decided to set up my own business and have established Cars 4 Hire as an independent car hire company. I believe the company can offer you a first class reliable, personalised and trouble free car rental service.

Our car hire rates include:

Super Damage Waiver, Super Theft Waiver, Airport Surchage, Tourism Levy, and Unlimeted KM's.

Our car hire rates exclude:

Contract fee (R55 per rental), Fuel, One-Way Fee, Additional Driver (R220), Delivery and Collection Fee, Baby Seats (R270), Claim Handling Fee (R395), Fine Handling Fee, After Hours Delivery/Collection, GPS Rental (R79)

About Our Partners

 

We have worked closely with several of the larger car rental companies and have been able to secure excellent working relationships with them. By working with the bigger companies we have been able to secure a wide range of vehicles which are not older than 2 years at affordable all inclusive rates. Our team at Cars 4 Hire are able to cater for any car rental requirement you might need throughout South Africa. We are able to arrange vehicles at any major airport and from any downtown location within the country. We are also able to offer car rental in Namibia and selected vehicles are permitted to cross into certain bordering countries with our prior consent.
